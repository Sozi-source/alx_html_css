Designing Diet Plans and Nutrition Consultancy

At Boresha Afya Rescue Centre, I am developing personalized diet plans to address the unique nutritional needs of individuals in recovery. This includes working with vulnerable populations such as malnourished children and adults with chronic illnesses. I provide tailored nutrition consultancy, focusing on restoring health, promoting well-being, and fostering sustainable dietary habits. My role also involves training caregivers to ensure consistent implementation of these plans.
